Dragon Ball Characters

Explore the legendary heroes and villains that make up the Dragon Ball universe!

Original Dragon Ball Series (Pre-Z)

Son Goku

Starts as a child with a tail, orange gi, and Power Pole. Often seen with his Nyoibo (Power Pole) and flying on Kinto-Un (Flying Nimbus).

Bulma

Blue/purple hair, often in futuristic outfits. A genius inventor and one of Goku's first friends on his journey.

Master Roshi (Kame-Sennin)

Old man with sunglasses, turtle shell, and beard. The martial arts master who trains Goku and Krillin.

Yamcha

Bandit with scar, later becomes a fighter in a green/yellow outfit. Initially Goku's enemy before becoming an ally.

Oolong

Small, pig-like shapeshifter. Can transform for up to five minutes at a time before needing a break.

Pu'ar

Blue cat-like creature, Yamcha's companion. Has the ability to shapeshift into various forms.

Chi-Chi

First appears as a young girl in armor (later becomes Goku's wife). Daughter of the Ox King.

Krillin

Bald, short martial artist (initially Goku's rival). Becomes Goku's best friend and loyal companion.

Tien Shinhan

Three-eyed martial artist in a white/blue outfit. Former student of the Crane School and rival to Goku.

Chiaotzu

Small, pale, doll-like fighter (Tien's partner). Has psychic abilities including telekinesis.

Piccolo (King Piccolo & Jr.)

Green-skinned Namekian (first as a villain, later an ally). Initially Goku's enemy, later becomes a mentor to Gohan.
